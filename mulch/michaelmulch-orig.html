The double-shredded bark much is made of 100% hardwood bark that is purchased from local sawmills and then shredded twice (How it gets its name double-shredded bark mulch).

Double-shredded bark mulch tends to be insect resistant because it is more solid–making it difficult for bugs to digest. Its light weight and lack of wood appearance make double-shredded bark mulch visually appealing.

Our double-shredded bark mulch is sold by the cubic yard.

Delivery Fees

Our delivery fees are based on radius and number of trips. Our dump truck holds 8 cubic yards of mulch in one trip. So if you order more than 8 cubic yards of mulch, we’re going to have to make more than one trip.

We charge a $60 delivery fee within 10 miles of zip code 22031.

For 11 — 20 miles, the delivery fee is $75.

For 2+0 miles and for deliveries in Washington DC, please call for delivery pricing 703.972.5171.

For more information and to place your order, please call 703.972.5171.

Capacity

We specialize in smaller deliveries between 1 and 8 cubic yards. Most companies have a minimum delivery of 6 cubic yards. Our minimum is 1 cubic yard or 1 ton of material.

Delivery Policy

Our dump trucks deliver BULK products and are not the large commercial trucks that bring huge loads but are residential delivery trucks.

Our policy is to deliver the bulk product on the driveway. In some situations, we can deliver off the driveway. It requires extremely dry and hard ground (no muddy or wet areas) but is left to the discretion of the driver. Please assume that the bulk product will be delivered on your driveway where there are no overhead obstructions.
