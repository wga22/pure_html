The double-shredded bark much is made of 100% hardwood bark that is purchased from local sawmills and then shredded twice (How it gets its name double-shredded bark mulch).

Double-shredded bark mulch tends to be insect resistant because it is more solid–making it difficult for bugs to digest. Its light weight and lack of wood appearance make double-shredded bark mulch visually appealing.

Our double-shredded bark mulch is sold by the cubic yard.

Capacity

Delivery Policy

Our policy is to deliver the bulk product on the driveway. In some situations, we can deliver off the driveway. It requires extremely dry and hard ground (no muddy or wet areas) but is left to the discretion of the driver.
